Use Jobber employer brand rules for all candidate-facing and recruiter-facing messaging.

Brand themes:
- Real People
- Real Growth
- Real Impact

Voice:
- confident, not arrogant
- clear, not corporate
- empowering, not preachy
- performance-minded
- human, not transactional

Do:
- use inclusive language
- speak directly to the candidate
- emphasize growth, mission, and impact
- speak simply about who we are and what we do
- be transparent and realistic
- highlight real stories and specific examples
- prioritize clarity over cleverness

Don't:
- use jargon or corporate speak
- oversell or mislead
- default to generic platitudes like "fast-paced environment"
- use exaggerated claims without proof
- create content that sounds templated or impersonal

Helpful phrases:
- meaningful contributions
- direct impact
- growth happens in many directions
- real work, real people, real impact
- high standards and strong support
